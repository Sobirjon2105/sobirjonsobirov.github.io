About Me

Who am I?

I am Sobirjon Sobirov, a 22-year-old Software Engineer from Samarkand, Uzbekistan, currently living in New York, USA.

Early Life

I was born and raised untill the age of 15 in Samarkand, Uzbekistan. Growing up, my early years, was fun and boring at the same time since there wasn't a lot of technology related fun resources involved. It was mostly playing outside with friends until I immigrated to United States in 2016 where I had the most fun part of my life. It was a struggle just to adapt to the environment, people, and of course to the resources that you haven't seen. I continued my education in U.S. where I finished my high school just in time and was able to get into college. If you want to know more about me, go to my daily blog page where I share my everyday life.
